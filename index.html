👋🏽 Hi there! I'm Femi — an urban strategist, policy analyst, and geospatial technologist passionate about equitable development and sustainable infrastructure. I bring systems thinking and spatial intelligence to global development challenges.

About Me

I hold dual master’s degrees in Urban Planning and Public Policy from Harvard University. Through roles at the United Nations, Harvard, and the Lincoln Institute, I’ve developed impactful strategies for carbon markets, land policy, and climate-smart cities — blending spatial analysis, policy design, and community engagement.

I'm also the founder of Nexus Engineering & Planning, a platform advancing geospatial solutions in Africa and beyond. I enjoy building tools, stories, and spaces that make knowledge more accessible and action more collaborative.
