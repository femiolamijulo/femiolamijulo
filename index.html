Hi, welcome to my website :) I am a Dev Degree Intern at Shopify in a Backend Engineering placement, and to be moving to Applied Machine Learning Engineering in January 2025. I am in my fourth year of Bachelor in Computer Science program at Carleton University in the Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning stream. At the moment, I work at the Nesbitt Biology Research Lab as an Undergraduate Research Assistant and my current research focuses on improving concussion diagnosis with visual eye tracking and ocular EMG analysis. I got blessed with a few scholarships for academic and technical excellence during my undergraduate days; a few selected ones are the Google Generation Scholarship, Palantir Women in Tech Scholarship, Cadence Diversity in Tech Scholarship, and Ms. Algorithm - Best Female Data Starlet in Nigeria 2020. Another info you may want to know, I was a Medical Rehabilitation student at Obafemi Awolowo University in Nigeria and I left the degree after two years to pursue computer science. I have touched almost every tech domain partially or fully, but I still have a strong bias towards ML Research, AI & Healthcare.
